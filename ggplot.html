Performance Analysis

Rating Analysis

For the Rating Analysis plot, we can see that ratings of Delta are all much higher than those of UA and AA, which showes that people are more comfortable flying with Delta.

Delay Analysis

From the above two plots, we can see that diverted actual elapsed time, diverted arrive delay and diverted distance can cause longer delay time but they rarely happen. On the other hand, the most common reason for flight delay is still carrier delay and late aircraft delay.

The above two plots tell us that flight delay always happen from midnight to dawn. And no matter for departure delay or arrive delay, Delta always performs worse in this period of time than the other two airlines.

From the above plot, we can see that flights tend to delay more at the beginning and the end of a week. United Airline performs worse than the other two airlines and departure time could always be delayed more than arrive time.

This plot can tell us lots of information. For example, United Airline could always be delayed in LAX. And if you want to go to Atlanta, the seemingly only choice for you is Delta.

Overbooking Analysis

The above plot tells us that the ratio of people denied boarding voluntarily from Delta is higher than UA and AA, which shows that oversale is really an issue in Delta. But the ratio of people denied boarding involuntarily for Delta is much lower than UA and AA, which means that Delta can handle this issue pretty well.
